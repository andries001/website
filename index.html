❓ Frequently Asked Questions

A bursary is a financial award given to students to help fund their studies. Unlike a loan, it does not need to be repaid. Bursaries are usually awarded based on academic merit, financial need, or a combination of both.

Each bursary has different requirements. Generally, you need to submit an application form, academic records, a motivation letter, and sometimes references. Check each bursary's specific requirements on our Services page.

Yes! You can apply for as many bursaries as you qualify for. This increases your chances of receiving funding. Just make sure you meet the requirements for each one.

Most bursaries open for applications between August and January for the following academic year. We recommend starting your research at least 6 months before you plan to study.
