📖 About BursaryBridge

Our story: BursaryBridge is a purpose-driven platform founded to address the information gap that prevents talented South African students from pursuing higher education. We aggregate verified funding opportunities from corporations, government, and non-profits.

🎯 Our Mission

To democratise access to education funding by providing a simple, reliable, and up-to-date bursary portal — empowering youth to build their future.

🌟 Our Vision

To become South Africa's most trusted bursary ecosystem, bridging the gap between students and funders, and increasing graduate success rates.

👥 Target Audience

  • High school learners (Grade 11 & 12)
  • University & TVET college students
  • Unemployed youth seeking skills funding

✅ Trusted by 20+ partner organisations and 3000+ students across SA.
